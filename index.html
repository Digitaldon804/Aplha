Step-by-Step ESP8266 Firmware Installation Guide
  • Select Your Firmware: Choose the Alpha Gadgets v2.7.2 option above.
  • Connect Board: Plug in your ESP8266 board to your PC via USB.
  • Initiate Connection: Click "Connect" and select the appropriate serial port of your ESP.
  • Flash Firmware: On the Device Dashboard, click "Install" to begin the flashing process.
Accessing the Advanced Web Serial Monitor
  • Select Your Firmware: Ensure the correct firmware is selected above.
  • Connect Board: Plug in your ESP8266 board via USB.
  • Initiate Connection: Click "Connect" and choose the ESP's serial port.
  • Open Console: On the Device Dashboard, click "Logs and Console" to view serial output.
